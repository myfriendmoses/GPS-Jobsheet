Mobile Installation Required

To ensure total offline availability and continuous background auto-saving during pilot transit operations, this application must be added to your mobile home screen first.

Apple iOS (Safari) Instructions:

  1. Tap the browser Share button panel icon (square with upward arrow) at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Scroll down the layout list and tap 'Add to Home Screen'.
  3. Confirm the name and tap Add in the top right corner.

Android (Chrome / Edge) Instructions:

  1. Tap the browser three-dot menu settings icon in the top right corner.
  2. Look down the menu options list and tap 'Add to Home screen' or 'Install app'.
  3. Confirm the prompt, and the app icon will appear on your device.

Once added, simply close this browser tab and launch the new icon from your home screen to unlock full workspace access.
