App Installation Required

To ensure total offline availability and continuous background auto-saving during pilot transit operations, this application must be added to your mobile home screen first.

iOS Installation
1
Tap the Share button in the browser navigation bar.
2
Scroll down the menu list and select Add to Home Screen.
3
Tap Add in the top right corner. Launch from your home screen.
Android Installation
1
Tap the Menu button (three vertical dots ) in the top-right of your browser.
2
Select Install app or Add to Home screen.
